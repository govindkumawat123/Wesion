Limited Supply:With a total supply of 1.25 billion tokens, the availability is finite. However, there are several ways to access tokens:

  • • Community Sale: Participate in our community sale to purchase tokens early.
  • • Staking Rewards: Earn tokens by staking your existing holdings.
  • • Marketplace Transactions: Earn tokens through transactions and activities on our marketplace.
  • • Airdrops and Incentives: Sign up for updates to participate in token airdrops and other incentive programs.

What Happens When All Tokens Are Used? Once the total supply of tokens is in circulation, the focus shifts to the secondary market.

  • Users can buy and sell tokens from each other, and the value of tokens may increase due to their limited supply.
  • Additionally, our burn mechanism ensures that some tokens are permanently removed from circulation, potentially increasing the value of the remaining tokens.
